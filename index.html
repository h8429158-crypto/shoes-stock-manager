About Steemlite

Since 1998, we've been a footwear manufacturer and wholesaler — making quality footwear in India at factory-direct prices, and shipping it in bulk to retailers and distributors across the country.

Everything is produced in-house, so we control quality at every stage and keep prices honest. From everyday slippers to performance sports shoes, our range is wide, our designs stay fresh, and our quality stays consistent order after order.

New retailer or established distributor — bulk orders are always welcome, and custom / OEM manufacturing is available on request.

Frequently Asked

What retailers usually ask before placing a bulk order.

What's your Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?

Our MOQ starts at 50 pairs per style for new retailers, with better per-pair pricing at higher volumes. Mix and match styles within a category is welcome.

What are your payment terms?

Typically 50% advance to confirm the order, with the balance due before dispatch. We can discuss terms for repeat and larger orders.

Can I get samples before ordering in bulk?

Yes — message us on WhatsApp with the styles you're interested in and we'll arrange samples so you can check quality before committing to a bulk order.

How long does dispatch take?

Most orders are packed and dispatched within 7–10 working days of confirming designs and quantities, depending on order size.

Do you offer custom or private-label (OEM) manufacturing?

Yes — we manufacture to your specifications, including your own branding, on request. Share your requirements on WhatsApp and we'll take it from there.

Which cities do you deliver to?

We ship pan-India to retailers and distributors in 50+ cities. Message us your location and we'll confirm delivery timelines for your city.
